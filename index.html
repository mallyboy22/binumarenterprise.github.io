About Us

BINUMAR ENTERPRISE is an Engineering, Consulting and Contracting Firm, duly registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission of Nigeria to undertake civil engineering construction jobs, provides and implement professional engineering design services for clients. It was incorporated on the 19th of December 2019 under the Companies Ordinance Act (Cap 32) as a Private Limited Liability Company with RC Number: 1643212. NEW BALANCE CORPORATE SERVICES LTD was created as a limited liability company. The company is an indigenous company; which is owned by Nigerian and Nigerian only. Our services and business activities include: Civil Engineering Construction, Project Management & Consultancy, Civil/Structural design, Electrical and Mechanical Design, Road Construction and Rehabilitation.
